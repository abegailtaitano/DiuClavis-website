ABOUT US:

William Taitano, professionally known as Diu Clavis, is a freelance licensed nail technician that specializes in natural nail care, gel extensions, and nail art as of February 2021. Growing up, he always envisioned finding a career that had a unique creative outlet, but something he could continue to build from and evolve the longer he is involved within the beauty industry. His main goal with nails are guaranteed flawless results, reliability, and make sure that customer satisfaction is of upmost importance. Our hands is one of the first things people notice when communicating, and to be able to give that confidence and make people feel beautiful is all that matters.
